Numbers

Arria specialises in converting numerical data to natural language, so number usage is central to what we do.

Unfortunately, referring to numbers can be tricky. In some cases, you should write numbers as words; in others, you should use numerals. To ensure consistency throughout our documentation, follow the rules below.

General rule

In general, you should spell out whole numbers from zero through nine, and use numerals for 10 or greater.

CORRECT There are four project types.
INCORRECT There are 4 project types.
CORRECT There are 165 ATL functions.
INCORRECT There are one hundred and sixty-five ATL functions.

However, there are notable exceptions to this rule:

  • There are cases in which you should always use words, even for numbers 10 and above.
  • There are cases in which you should always use numerals, even for numbers zero through nine.

When to always use words

In these cases, always use words to express numbers, even numbers 10 and above:

CASE EXAMPLE
When the number starts a sentence Fifteen branches exceeded the sales target.
When the number is ordinal Donald Trump was the forty-fifth president.
When the number expresses a fraction Almost three-fourths of the earth's surface is water.
When two numbers must appear together The file contains twenty 100 MB files.
When using numbers imprecisely for emphasis Studio has hundreds of built-in functions.

Use hyphens when writing out compound numbers. See Spelling compound numbers for guidance.

When to always use numerals

In these cases, always use numerals to express numbers, even numbers zero through nine:

CASE EXAMPLE
When the number precedes a unit of measurement The file size limit is 200 MB.
When the number is used in a mathematical equation 2 + 2 = 4
When the number is decimal Average monthly sales were 2.5 units.
When the number expresses a percentage value Total sales increased by 4%.
When the number expresses a ratio The monitor has a 4:3 aspect ratio.
When the number refers to a document page or section Refer to Section 2 of the user manual.
When the number expresses age My daughter is 3 years old.
When the number expresses a date The webinar is scheduled for July 1, 2021.
When the number expresses a time The webinar starts at 3:30 PM.
When the number is used in a currency expression Each unit of Retine costs $5.
When instructing the reader to enter a numeric value Set the DECIMAL PLACES parameter to 2.

In most cases, use commas in numerals with four or more digits. See How to use commas in numbers for guidance.

Spelling compound numbers

Use hyphens when spelling out numbers 21 through 99.

CORRECT Thirty-four people attended the conference.
INCORRECT Thirty four people attended the conference.

A round number with a value greater than 99 does not require a hyphen.

CORRECT Two hundred people attended the conference.
INCORRECT Two-hundred people attended the conference.

The hyphens-in-numbers rule applies even when the compound number is preceded by other numbers that don't require hyphens.

CORRECT Two hundred thirty-four people attended the conference.
INCORRECT Two-hundred thirty-four people attended the conference.

How to use commas in numbers

In general, include commas as thousands separators for numbers 1,000 and above.

CORRECT The company has 1,035 employees in Canada.
INCORRECT The company has 1035 employees in Canada.
CORRECT The London branch achieved a Net Profit of £1,349,824.57.
INCORRECT The London branch achieved a Net Profit of £1349824.57.

However, never use commas in a four-digit numeral expressing a year.

CORRECT Arria NLG doubled its revenue in 2021.
INCORRECT Arria NLG doubled its revenue in 2,021.

Likewise, omit the comma from a four-digit numeral expressing a screen resolution.

CORRECT The monitor's resolution is 7880 x 4320 pixels.
INCORRECT The monitor's resolution is 7,880 x 4,320 pixels.
